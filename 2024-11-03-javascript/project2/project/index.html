About my project

In this project, I built a website that offers valuable information and reports from the world of virtual trading. The primary goal was to develop a user-friendly platform that aggregates up-to-date and relevant data for traders and enthusiasts alike. Virtual trading has become an increasingly popular and dynamic field in recent years, and I aimed to provide accessible, practical tools for navigating the market. On the website, users can access a variety of information, including:

  • Currency Status: Real-time data on the value of leading digital currencies.
  • Prices and History: Graphs and data displays showing the price fluctuations of currencies over time.

This project allows users to gain insights into the market, understand trends, and plan their next trading steps more intelligently.
